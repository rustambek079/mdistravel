    From April to June of the qurrent year, short-term trainings and advanced courses were held for the employees and specialists in the sphere of tourism and hospitality in the above-mentioned regions. Active work is conducted to develop catalogs, tour packages and brochures for the mentioned regions. This site is a platform for highlighting the information about the tourism potential of the Bostanlyk, Parkent, Okhangaran districts, and Angren city of the Tashkent region, which can serve as an effective information tool for both local and foreign tourists wishing to visit these regions of our country. The aim of the site is to cover news and modern trends in the field of tourism and hospitality, as well as the constant replenishment of information about the objects of historical and cultural heritage. Our mission is to develop the tourism potential of the attached regions and attract a greater flow of local and foreign tourists through a wide coverage of the features and cultural heritage of the regions, as well as replenishing the platform with the latest news happening in the world. Also MDIS Tashkent has a highly reputable school of Tourism and Hospitality, which runs its successful Tourism Lab and Business and Tourism Innovations Center. The school is led by highly skilled international and national tourism professionals both in industry and academia, balancing and integrating international experience in local business environment. The state-of-the-art facilities that is not found in any other institution across Uzbekistan makes Tourism and Hospitality School stand out in terms of delivering theoretical and practical knowledge and hands on experience right into the industry itself. In partnership with Bangor University, the School offers and supervises two programmes under Tourism and Hospitality School. Modules include a blend of Business Management and Travel, Tourism and Hospitality, including Introduction to Travel and Tourism in Uzbekistan along with Principles of Business Management and Introduction to Management and Financial Accounting.
